About

This website is designed to be a tracking page for my project to take a photo of someone from every country in the world.

I've tried a few different ways of tracking the project, and although this will involve more work, it's the best way to visualise it.

If you'd like to follow the progress please feel free! I'll update this for my own benefit as I go along.

I ask if you do follow along, please don't share any of the photos as they are obviously photos of people and I don't want their photos to be distributed without their consent.

Thanks for checking in on the project!
